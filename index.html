Pages are a canvas for the written word. Selecting the layout and dimension of pages is critical when planning the location and flow of content.  Print Centuries of printing has resulted in countless paper sizes. In the United States, common dimensions range from folio, quatro, and octavo. In the United Kingdom, the A-format and B-format are most often used. No matter the dimensions, we have the capacity to print every size.   DigitalLayouts are especially important in digital publishing because of varying screen dimension. A layout on a larger desktop display may not translate onto a smaller mobile display. We create multiple layouts to accommodate mobile, tablet, and desktop screen dimensions so that the content is appealing regardless of the device.
