Pages are the canvas for written word. Selecting the layout and dimension of pages is critical when planning the location and flow of content.  Print Centuries of printing has resulted in countless paper sizes. In the United States, common dimensions range from folio, quatro, and octavo. In the United Kingdom, the A-format and B-format are most often used. Regardless of the dimensions, we have the capacity to print them all.   DigitalLayouts are especially important in digital publishing because of varying screen dimension. A large, desktop layout may not translate well on a small, mobile layout. We create multiple layouts to accommodate mobile, tablet, and desktop screen dimensions so that the content looks stunning regardless of the device.
