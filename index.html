Arts and Media
Depending on the subject matter and platform, some works require extensive arts and media. Diagrams for a textbookbook, recipe videos for a cookbook, or drawings for a nonfiction, Vanadium Press produces in-house or commissions art and media. We always communicate with clients to ensure the art and media meet their vision objectives. Creative Commons and Public DomainTo reduce clients’ project costs, we also provide the option to use open source and public domain art and media for commerical and non-commericial publishing.  Regardless of the method, we’ll ensure the art and media supports the written content in an engaging and pleasant manner.
Sizes and Layouts
Pages are the canvas for the content. Selecting the dimension of pages is critical when planning the location and flow of content.  Print Millenia of printing has resulted in countless of paper sizes that vary by region. In the United States, common dimensions range from folio, quatro, and octavo. In the United Kingdom, the A-format and B-format are most often used. Regardless of the paper size and book dimension, Vanadium Press has the capacity to print them all.   DigitalLayouts are especially important in digital publishing because of varying screen dimension. A large layout for desktop may not translate on a small mobile display. We create multiple layouts to accomdate mobile, tablet, and desktop screen dimensions.
Design requires precision. Our team of talented artists and graphic designers are able to create a design that is both attractive and uncomplicated, but is furthermore tasteful and unique. From the choice of typeface to the selection of art, our attention to detail will ensure the work is stunning.
